IMF lending at an all-time high?

IMF lending to economically troubled countries between 1984 and 2022.

IMF Lending relative to GDP.

The graph represents IMF lending as a proportion of US GDP and so shows relative size of debt over time. If debt is constant but GDP is rising, the debt will be easier to pay back.

In nominal terms, 2022 is the year with the most lending. However, once expressed as a fraction of GDP, we see 1984 had the highest burden of debt, making it the 'riskiest' year.
