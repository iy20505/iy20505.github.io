Has bitcoin failed as an inflation hedge?


monkey buys btc to hedge against 10% inflation...

btc falls 75%

Bitcoin's value largely comes from it existing as an inflation hedge. Fiat money, such as the dollar, can be printed infinitely which dilutes the value of each individual dollar and increases the price of things; otherwise known as inflation. We therefore seek something scarce to protect (or hedge) against this, like gold, prime real estate, and more recently bitcoin.

Amidst the euphoria of 2021, bitcoin seemed unstoppable with its powerful narrative of protecting against inflation and government confiscation. However, the last 12 months have led many to question bitcoinâ€™s validity as a hedge. If inflation is up, why is btc down?
